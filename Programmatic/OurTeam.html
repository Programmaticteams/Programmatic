Our Team

Our instructors are high school students who are experienced in both teaching and computer science. They bring a fresh and innovative approach to the classroom, creating an engaging and interactive learning experience for all participants. Their passion for education and expertise in the field make them excellent mentors, guiding students through the intricacies of programming and computer science concepts. With their dedication and knowledge, our instructors inspire and empower learners, fostering a love for coding and technology that will stay with them throughout their educational journey.
