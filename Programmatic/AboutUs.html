What is Programmatic

Programmatic is a nonprofit organization committed to providing extensive free online education in Scratch, Python, Raspberry Pi, and Unity. Through Google Meet, we connect with students from diverse backgrounds and geographical locations, offering comprehensive courses taught by experienced high school student instructors. Our mission is to empower learners with valuable computer science skills, foster a global community of knowledge-sharing, and make education accessible to all, regardless of financial constraints. Join us in our journey to create a future where technology is a force for positive change and empowerment.

Our mission

Our mission is to nurture digital literacy among students while equipping them with skills crucial for thriving in an era defined by computers.

Future goals

Our future goals are To expand our teaching into other languages besides English and teaching a wider variety of programming languages.
