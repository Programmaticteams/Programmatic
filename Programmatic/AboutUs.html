Programmatic

what is Programmatic

We are a nonprofit organization that provides extensive free online education in Scratch, Python, Raspberry Pi, and Unity. We teach classes through google meet, which helps us connect with students from diverse backgrounds and geographical locations. Our instructors are high school students experienced in teaching and computer science.

Our mission

Our mission is to nurture digital literacy among students while equipping them with skills crucial for thriving in an era defined by computers.

Future goals

Our future goals are To expand our teaching into other languages besides English and teaching a wider variety of programming languages.
